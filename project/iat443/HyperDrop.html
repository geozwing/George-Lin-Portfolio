HYPER DROPGENERATIVE ARTANDREW ANG & GEORGE LIN12 FEB 2023TITLECATERGORYDESIGNERSDATE"HyperDrop," is a mesmerizing art piece that showcases the seamless morphing of a 3D cat object and a human object in a vivid, hyper-dimensional environment. A 3D cat object and a human object gradually merge and transform in response to the pulsating rhythm of the music. As the bassline intensifies, the objects' features morph and stretch, blurring the boundaries between species and form. Meanwhile, the atmosphere itself seems to pulse with life, its colours and textures morphing in harmony with the music. The overall effect is an immersive, multi-sensory experience that transports the viewer to a realm of otherworldly beauty and transformation.
