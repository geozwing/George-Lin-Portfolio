A 3D cat object and a human object gradually merge and transform in response to the pulsating rhythm of the music. As the bassline intensifies, the objects' features morph and stretch, blurring the boundaries between species and form. Meanwhile, the atmosphere itself seems to pulse with life, its colours and textures morphing in harmony with the music.
