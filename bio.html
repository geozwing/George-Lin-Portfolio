I am George Lin, a third-year undergraduate student at Simon Fraser University majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology with Design Concentration. My goal is to be a successful visual designer in the media, film or video game industry and getting my works acknowledged by the rest of the world due to my interest in 3D modeling and animations. In order to achieve that, I must produce designs that are competitively unique and creatively stands out to the public eye. However, the path to such creative ambition is arduous, yet I never stop giving up on something that I am passionate at.
